Professional Experience

    Present
    Research Assistant
    Florida International University, Miami, FL, USA
    • Curate, organize and develop analytic pipelines to process and analyze imaging and tabulated data from thousands of individuals from big data consortiums (ABCD, ABIDE, ADNI, ENIGMA, and HCP).
    • Perform signal processing and denoising data from millions of voxels per structural and functional 3D/4D brain image.
